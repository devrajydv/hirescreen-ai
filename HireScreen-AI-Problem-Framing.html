Problem Statement

Core Problem

Small and mid-sized technology companies receive hundreds of unstructured resume files for every open role, but lack the HR staff, time, and budget to screen them effectively. The result is a broken hiring pipeline where great candidates are missed due to human fatigue, top talent accepts competing offers during slow scheduling cycles, and HR managers spend the majority of their week on low-judgment administrative tasks instead of strategic hiring decisions. The problem is not a lack of candidates ‚Äî it is a lack of capacity to evaluate them fairly and fast.
